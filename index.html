Analysis:

  • The SOV is ~35% faster when the data is larger than the L2 cache and this performance improvement grows exponentially.
  • std::vector is generally faster at lower sizes (due to the overhead of my unoptimised implementation).
Conclusion:
  • Some gains can be made with an SOA, particularly when your data fields have interdependencies. (Potential vetorisation opportunities too)
  • However for data under 100KB, the SOA/SOV approach doesn't yield notable speed gains.
